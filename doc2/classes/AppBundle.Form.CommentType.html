\AppBundle\Form

\AppBundle\FormCommentType

Defines the form used to create and manipulate blog comments. Although in this case the form is trivial and we could build it inside the controller, a good practice is to always define your forms as classes.

See http://symfony.com/doc/current/book/forms.html#creating-form-classes
